Research

My research focuses on sample-efficient reinforcement learning with world models, safe/uncertainty-aware policy learning, and spatial data mining (colocation pattern mining). I work at the intersection of AI and healthcare applications.

  • World models & representation learning for embodied RL
  • Safe RL, uncertainty estimation, and deployment
  • Colocation pattern mining & geospatial analytics

Teaching

CSC-724 Applied Reinforcement Learning

This course aims to leverage reinforcement learning algorithms in solving sequential decision-making problems or use-cases. Topics include policy gradients and Qlearning, and real-world applications using open frameworks

CSC 547 Artificial Intelligence

This course provides a foundational understanding of Artificial Intelligence (AI) concepts, techniques, and applications. You will explore knowledge representation, search algorithms, problem-solving, logical inference, and AI programming languages. Additionally, the course will delve into automated planning, machine learning, and the ethical and social impacts of AI.

CSC-792 Topics: Advance Data Mining

This course explores cutting-edge techniques for discovering patterns and insights in large, complex datasets. Building on foundational data mining concepts, students will delve into advanced algorithms for pattern mining.
