Research

My research focuses on reinforcement learning, energy-efficient AI systems, and scalable pattern mining over spatial and large-scale data. I work at the intersection of learning theory, sustainable AI, and real-world AI deployment.

  • Reinforcement learning and decision-making systems
  • Carbon-aware and energy-efficient AI (AI-CARE)
  • Spatial data mining and colocation pattern analysis
  • Safe and uncertainty-aware AI deployment

Education: Ph.D. (IIIT-Delhi, 2024), M.Tech. (NIT Hamirpur, 2012), B.Tech. (JNTU Hyderabad, 2010).

Teaching

This course aims to leverage reinforcement learning algorithms in solving sequential decision-making problems or use-cases. Topics include policy gradients and Qlearning, and real-world applications using open frameworks

CSC-547 Artificial Intelligence

This course provides a foundational understanding of Artificial Intelligence (AI) concepts, techniques, and applications. You will explore knowledge representation, search algorithms, problem-solving, logical inference, and AI programming languages.
