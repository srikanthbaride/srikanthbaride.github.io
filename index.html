Research

My research focuses on sample-efficient reinforcement learning with world models, safe/uncertainty-aware policy learning, and spatial data mining (colocation pattern mining). I work at the intersection of AI and healthcare applications.

  • World models & representation learning for embodied RL
  • Safe RL, uncertainty estimation, and deployment
  • Colocation pattern mining & geospatial analytics
